        of the Cauchy-Green strain tensor, cf.\ equation
        \eqref{eq:cauchygreencharacteristics}. Along the $\vct{\xi}_{2}$ and
        $\vct{\xi}_{1}$ axes, the stretch factors are $\sqrt{\lambda_{2}}$ and
        $\sqrt{\lambda_{1}}$, respectively.}
